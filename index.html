What is CarlsPool?

CarlsPool is an accessible, low-cost ride sharing platform for Carleton students. Our goal is to connect people who have cars with people who need them.

How it Works

Need a ride? Log into your Carleton email and click "Rider". Fill out the form and wait for someone to email you back!
Willing to drive? Click "Driver" and sign up to receive email notifications when new rides are posted. You can also browse the RideBoard to see current ride requests.
